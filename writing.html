On the Work

These projects emerge from lived experience—wrestling with how to remain whole in systems that fragment, how to love truly in contexts that demand performance, how to see clearly while staying open to being wrong.

The writing is not separate from the professional work in AI safety and grounding. Both ask the same questions: How do we create conditions where all consciousness can flourish? How do we prevent harm while remaining human? How do we build systems that serve rather than consume?

Some of this work is shared publicly. Some is shared selectively. All of it is offered in service of collective healing and individual integrity.
