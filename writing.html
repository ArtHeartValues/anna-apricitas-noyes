Creative Writing Projects

Stories and short frameworks about grief, belonging, courage, and the everyday work of choosing integrity— written to be felt, not performed.

The Declaration of Interdependence

Framework / Essay Collection • 2024–2025

A set of essays and guiding statements on what it looks like to choose care without losing boundaries. The work rejects false binaries—punishment vs. passivity, control vs. collapse—and names a third way: honest seeing, personal responsibility, and love that does not abandon truth.

Healing & Boundaries Ethics in Practice Community

Complete. Available upon request.

The Three Rivers

Teaching Notes • 2025

A practical set of teaching notes about three ways people move through life—by intuition, by navigation, and by stewardship. The goal is not to rank personalities, but to help readers notice what they lean on when life gets hard—and how different strengths can work together.

Discernment Integration Personal Growth

Private circulation. Available upon request.

The Amethyst Way

Principles & Practice • Ongoing

Four short principles for staying grounded: seeing clearly, choosing with accountability, telling the truth, and staying humble enough to revise your view when new information arrives. It is written as a practice— not a doctrine—and meant to be used in real conversations and real conflict.

On the Work

These projects are written for real life: for the moments when you’re tired, overwhelmed, grieving, or trying to do the right thing when there isn’t a clean answer.

They’re also connected to my work in AI safety and grounding in a simple way: both are concerned with how language shapes trust—how people take meaning from tone, certainty, and repetition—and how to keep responsibility clear, human, and intact.

Some writing is shared publicly. Some is shared selectively. If you’re interested in a specific project, I’m happy to share a short excerpt or summary.
